About myself

My name is Kevin Zhang. I come from a Chinese family. I was born in Milan, Italy in July 20th, 1995. My father is a salesman who keep running between Europe and China. I spent half of my childhood in Italy while other half in China. I am not the only child in the family, I have an elder brother and we’ve grew up together. Since I am the youngest, I can feel that my parents care more about me than my brother. Currently, I am an undergraduate student in University of Wisconsin-Madison, majoring in computer science. I spent most of my time on coding and debugging, and using my computer became a part of my life. I am skilled in the following languages: Java, HTML, CSS, Javascript, C.

Gaming experiences
